Smart Home Case
“Mary is a widow. She is 65 years old, overweight and has high blood pressure and cholesterol levels. Mary gets a new intelligent fridge. It comes with 4 temperature and 2 humidity sensors and is able to read, store and communicate RFID information on food packages. The fridge detects the presence of spoiled food and discovers and receives a diet plan to be monitored based on what food items Mary is consuming. An important part of Mary’s diet is to ensure minimum liquid intake. The intelligent fridge monitors Mary’s liquid consumption and notifies the emergency services in case the liquid intake consistently falls below minimum threshold.”

  1. The fridge shall communication and integrate itself with the smart home system.
  2. The fridge shall detect and communicate with packages
  3. The fridge shall detect spoiled food.
  4. The fridge shall monitor and adjust the diet plan.
  5. The system shall ensure a minimum liquid intake.
